This song was written as a mantra. A mantra for times like these. A mantra of self love. I want to extend this mantra to all of my brothers and sisters. I am you and you are me.

The atrocities I've seen across our nation and specifically in my backyard of Atlanta GA have sparked something deep in all of us.

We will be heard.
We will be seen.
We will not be silenced.
Black is Beautiful.
Black is worthy.
We are Black.

I love you thank you.

LaDonnis
